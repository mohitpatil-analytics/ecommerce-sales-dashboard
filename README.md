# 📊 E-Commerce Sales & Customer Analytics Dashboard

An interactive Business Intelligence dashboard built using **Power BI, MySQL, and DAX** to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and regional trends from retail transaction data.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project focuses on transforming raw sales data into actionable business insights through data cleaning, SQL analysis, KPI development, and interactive visualizations.

The dashboard helps stakeholders monitor sales performance, identify top-performing categories and customers, and evaluate profitability across different regions.

## 💡 Business Insights

- Technology category generated the highest revenue.
- West region contributed the largest share of sales.
- Overall profit margin was approximately 12.47%.
- A small group of customers generated a significant portion of total revenue.
- Monthly trend analysis helped identify sales growth patterns over time.
